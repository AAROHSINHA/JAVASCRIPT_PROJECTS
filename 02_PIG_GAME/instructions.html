HOW TO PLAY

1. Your Aim is to roll the dice and the number which comes on the dice gets added to your current score.

2. You can save the current score to the total score so that it remains safe. Once saved, your turn is over for the time being.

3. If the number 1 appear on the dice, your current score resets to zero

4. So it depends on you whenever to save the current score to the total score...or take risks..

5.First person to reach the total score of 100 wins!
